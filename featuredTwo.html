Dr Jekyll
and Mr Hyde Essay

This paper provides an analysis of the duality of Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde
and the relation of that duality to the Victorian era and the era of social media

I think the main relation this work has had with my work in the capstone course comes from the idea of trying to take an idea that is thought of in my head and having the ability to share that idea with others in a way that is understandable. Each projects require extensive documentation and resource gathering in order to complete that task. Each project required writing that would allow for someone who has little to no experience in the subject to be able to understand and interact with the work. Another aspect of both projects was the iterative approach to work, each version being done the first time, then being revised and edited over and over again to gain the best results.
